🔧 Debugging Tips (If Results Don't Match)

  • Unit Consistency: Are you mixing mm and meters? (e.g., Span in meters but I in mm⁴?)
  • Sign Convention: Is downward load positive or negative in your array?
  • UDL Conversion: For 2-node beams, UDL must be converted to equivalent nodal loads (wL/2 at each end)
  • Boundary Conditions: Pinned = [true, true, false], Roller = [false, true, false], Fixed = [true, true, true]
  • Global vs Local: Ensure transformation matrix T is correctly applied for angled elements
